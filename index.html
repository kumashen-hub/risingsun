Our Story

A Home Away From Home on the South Coast

House of the Rising Sun is situated approximately 60km South of Durban (just 45 minutes from Durban International Airport) on the beautiful South Coast of KwaZulu-Natal. Our property features luxurious 3 to 4 bedroom apartments plus individual rooms — all with ensuite bathrooms, magnificent sea views, DSTV, and air conditioning. With a total of 16 rooms, we cater to both holiday makers and business travellers.

Only 800m from Park Rynie Beach and 5 minutes from Scottburgh Beach, we offer the perfect blend of comfort, peace, and tranquility. Whether you are here for a family holiday, a diving adventure at Aliwal Shoal, or a business trip, we provide the ideal base for your KZN coastal experience.
